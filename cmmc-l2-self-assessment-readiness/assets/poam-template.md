## Notes on DoD Assessment Methodology Scoring

Per the DoD Assessment Methodology, point deductions are assigned as follows:

- **5 points:** Control not implemented, or implementation does not meet the intent of the requirement
- **3 points:** Control partially implemented, but significant gaps exist
- **1 point:** Control is implemented but has minor deficiencies

Each of the 110 requirements can result in a maximum deduction based on the severity of the gap. The goal is a score of 110 (all requirements met with no deductions).
